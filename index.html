Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build my website? +
It depends on the package. Starter takes 3 days, Professional takes 5 days, and Premium takes 7–10 days. I always keep you updated throughout the process.
How do I pay you? +
I accept M-Pesa payments. I typically ask for 50% upfront before starting and 50% on delivery. This is flexible — we can discuss what works for you.
Will my website work on mobile phones? +
Yes! Every website I build is fully mobile-responsive. It will look great and work perfectly on phones, tablets, and computers of all sizes.
What is Google My Business and why do I need it? +
Google My Business (GMB) is a free tool that lets your business appear on Google Maps and local search results. When someone searches "restaurant near me" or "barber shop Nairobi," a properly set up GMB listing makes your business show up — bringing you more customers automatically.
Can I update the website myself after it's done? +
Yes. I'll hand over all the source code files so you can make changes. I also offer affordable maintenance if you'd prefer me to handle updates.
Do you host the website too? +
I can host your website on GitHub Pages for free. A custom domain (like www.yourbusiness.com) costs around KSh 1,000–2,000/year extra.
